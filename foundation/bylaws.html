Bylaws

Article I: Name
The name of this organization shall be the AZT Foundation, referred to herein as the "Foundation."

Article II: Mission and Purpose
Our mission is to empower the youth to create positive change for future generations by fostering collaboration, innovation, and community engagement. Through grassroots initiatives and meaningful projects, we strive to address pressing global challenges, promote sustainability, and cultivate a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive. Together, we envision a brighter future for the next generation, built on the values of compassion, equity, and stewardship of our planet.

Article III: Board of Directors
Section 1: Composition
1.1 The Foundation shall have a Board of Directors (the "Board") composed of individuals committed to furthering the Foundation's mission.
1.2 The number of directors constituting the Board shall be determined by the Board, but shall not be fewer than [minimum number] and not more than [maximum number].

Section 2: Duties and Powers
2.1 The Board shall have the authority to govern the Foundation and oversee its activities.
2.2 The Board shall be responsible for hiring, evaluating, and, if necessary, terminating the Executive Director.
